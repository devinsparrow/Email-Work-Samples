Welcome to the community!
Office 365 Champions are professionals like you who are passionate about Office 365, excited to help peers, and ready to lead. In return for the great qualities you bring to the table, we want to keep you in the loop with the latest updates and insider views from Office 365.

This new community will connect you with other like-minded users, enable you to share and learn from each other, give you exclusive access to Microsoft product experts, and arm you with tools and resources that you can use to be a Champion for your peers at work.

Office 365 Champions Program Team

The latest from Office 365
As a Champion, you get the latest Office 365 news straight from the source. Read on for recent announcements, and be sure to check out more in our LinkedIn Group.

Expand your collaboration with guest access in Microsoft Teams
Microsoft Teams is the hub for teamwork in Office 365, helping you collaborate on documents and stay connected via chat, calls, and meetings. Recently, Teams rolled out guest access to all Office 365 commercial and education customers. That means that you can now expand your collaboration by using Teams with people from outside your organization.

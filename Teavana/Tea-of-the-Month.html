MANGO BLACK HURRICANE
 
Craft this sunny cocktail with Mango Black,
one of our top picks for June.
Join the Tea of the Month club
 
WHAT YOU NEED
 
1/2 cup Mango Black Tea
1/4 cup Belgian Rock Sugar
3/4 oz white rum
1/4 oz dark rum
3 oz passionfruit juice
1/4 oz fresh lime juice
1/4 oz fresh lemon juice
Perfectea® Maker (16 oz)
Chilled glass (8 oz)
Pitcher
 
HOW TO MAKE
 
Combine tea and sugar in your Perfectea® Maker.
Add hot water and steep for 3 minutes.
Fill a pitcher with ice.
Release tea into pitcher and strain out ice.
Fill a cocktail shaker with ice.
Add rums, juices and 3 oz of brewed tea.
Shake shake shake.
Strain cocktail into glass.
Enjoy!

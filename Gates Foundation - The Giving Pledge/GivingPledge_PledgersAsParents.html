  Each of us has committed to give over half of our wealth to philanthropy. The balance of our legacy, what and how we give to our children and grandchildren, is a topic on many of our minds.

Scott and Signe will host pledgers who want to delve into the questions on what and how our descendants will inherit from us. The format will be peer‑to‑peer group discussion enabling pledgers to share their questions, experiences, worries, and ideas. We will learn from one another about what has worked (or not worked) for each other’s unique families. Attendance is limited to pledgers and their spouses/partners.

Here are some of the questions we plan to explore together:
  How do we pass on values and promote philanthropic stewardship across generations?

How do we plan for wealth transfer? With what kind of limitations, if any? When and how should we share this with our children?

How do we inspire purpose and work ethic in our children and grandchildren?

What are ways to minimize sibling strife? How do we manage the differences between children?

What are ways that others have engaged their children in the family giving?
 
  Transportation from the Annual Gathering to our ranch will be provided. Individual pick-ups from the ranch to local airports will be coordinated by the Giving Pledge team.

Pledgers are welcome to stay after the session for an optional tour of the ranch’s holistic grazing operation that cultivates pasture-raised alpaca and Black Angus beef.
